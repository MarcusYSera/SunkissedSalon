Kacie Nguyen

Hi! I’m Kacie and I am the proud owner of Sunkissed Salon in the Bay Area. Since I pursued being a hairstylist at the age of 19, it was definitely not easy for me. There were times where I wanted to give up or quit, but I am glad I didn’t because 10 years later, I love my job and everyone I met along the way. The experience and the relationships that is built in this industry is unbelievable. I am very thankful for my clients and our hair dates because without the trust and time you spend during our sessions, my passion to build a team and salon would’ve never happened. Lastly, I had the pleasure to work and teach such hard working and dream driven ladies who I call my family, the #sunkissedgals.
