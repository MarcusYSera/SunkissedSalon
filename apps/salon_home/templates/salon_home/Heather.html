Heather

Hi! My name is Heather Nguyen. Ever since I was young I’ve always had a passion to be creative. I just never knew what I wanted to do with that creativity. I’ve tried my hand at illustrating, digital animation, baking pastries, and even nail art. But none of them gave me that drive for a career. Then I went back to school to try and become a hairstylist. BEST DECISION OF MY LIFE! I’ve never had a job where I wake up early and excite to go to work. My goals for our hairdates is to keep the integrity of your hair while making your color dreams come true! I’ve trained under Kacie Nguyen for the past two years and my favorite part of doing hair is the REVEAL. When you look in the mirror and smile, that’s honestly the best feeling ever. When I’ve empowered a person to feel beautiful! Can’t wait to meet you!
