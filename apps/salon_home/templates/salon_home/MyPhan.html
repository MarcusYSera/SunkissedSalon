Permanent Makeup Artist & Trainer

I specializes in all permanent makeup procedures (microblading, microshading, ombré, lips blush, and lash enhancement liner). I wanted to focus on a pattern and color that are simple, soft, natural, but at the same time frame the face and improve my client's overall look. My goals are to enhance and create the most natural semi-permanent makeup procedures and delivering my service with most comfort, care, and safety for all my clients. And, passing all my experience and skills to my students/future artists. At our studio, we: -follow high standard of sanitation and hygiene -use new and disposable needles and pens always -apply topical anesthetic cream for pain control -use pigments that are safe
