Welcome to our salon! We are glad you have chosen us to help you achieve your hair goals and hope that every encounter with our salon and staff exceeds your expectations. We know our clients have a choice in many different salons, so we try to go out of our way to deliver the level of hair care and compassion we would want for our lush locks. It’s the goal of each stylist to provide you with the best experience we can. Healthy hair may be our long-term goal, but achieving your desired cut and color is something we pride ourselves on. We provide a through consultation with each and every one of our clients to make sure that you have a piece of mind and trust that your hair is in good hands. Our salon is not a traditional salon people usually walk into. We are one huge family that help each other out. We make sure all clients feel welcomed and attended to, so your encounter is not just with your stylist but with everyone here. Our staff is fully trained in not only taking care of your hair at the salon but also helping and teaching you how to maintain your hair at home. Home hair care is one of the most important things about keeping your hair looking fresh and healthy until your next appointment. We carry Fanola and Oribe and we teach our clients how to use products properly. We’re looking forward to all our hair dates! See you soon!
